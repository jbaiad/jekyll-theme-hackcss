About Me

Hi there! I'm Josh and I'm a quantitative researcher and developer.

I currently work on AQR's risk parity research team. I previously worked as a consultant for onTREND, a trend-following hedge fund based out of Toronto, where I designed and led the development of their proprietary trading platform. I've since been an advisor to onTREND, providing direction for long-term technical and research goals.

I was educated at Carnegie Mellon University, where I studied mathematics, logic, and computer science.
